Pricing

Our Plans

Our goal is to make CommunityHERO available and affordable for any and every organization, regardless of size or wealth. Our app is available for free, and Community sponsorship pricing is based upon a combination of active users, or Members, and Subscribers. Subscribers are people from the CommunityHERO network who are either members of other communities, or have downloaded the free app.
